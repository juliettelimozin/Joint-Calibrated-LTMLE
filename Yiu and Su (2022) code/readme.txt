Code_for_1st_simulation_scenario1.R: R code for Scenario 1 (without censoring/dropout) in the first simulation study of Yiu & Su (2020)

Code_for_1st_simulation_scenario2.R: R code for Scenario 2 (with censoring/dropout) in the first simulation study of Yiu & Su (2020)

Code_for_2nd_simulation.R: R code for the second simulation study of Yiu & Su (2020), comparing the proposed calibrated approach with the MLE approach and the Covariate Balancing Propensity Score  approach by  Imai and Ratkovic (2015, JASA)
